Online Ride Sharing

The Urban Riders app interface showcases a ride-sharing service designed to connect users with available transportation options in urban areas. The app allows users to book rides by selecting their starting point and destination, as seen with the route from Mirpur-1 to Dhanmondi. It provides a list of available ride options, including different car types, each with a capacity (e.g., 4 passengers) and a corresponding fare (e.g., $67). The map displays the user's current location (Mirpur-1) and nearby areas, helping users visualize their route and the surrounding neighborhoods. The app also includes navigation links such as Home, Destination, Blog, and Contact, enabling users to explore additional features, access support, or learn more about the service.
